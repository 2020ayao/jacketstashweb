Frequently Asked Questions

How do we keep your jacket secure?

It's simple. JacketStash uses a secure backend to process all check in and check out events. Your unique coat id number corresponds to a hanger we own, so that only you get back your coat once checked in.

Why is the price so low?

Our mission is to provide affordable, secure checkins for college town bars. We make our profit on repeat customers, and have knocked the price down wherever we could.

What happens if I forget to check out?

Because we have your name down in our secure database, we'll make a note of your name and return it to the bar you were at. We'll also send an email toward the end of the night if we notice you haven't checked out yet.

How are we keeping jackets secured?

We vet bars before going into partnership with them on the basis of security, staff, and overall performance. The bars will be responsible for the security and safety of the coats once stored inside. Coats are kept behind a counter, and only staff members will be allowed to access hangers.
